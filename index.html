About Us

Creating a fair, transparent, and simple marketplace where local communities thrive.

Our Mission

At Mobitrons, our mission is to restore fairness and freedom in local commerce. We believe every customer should have the power to choose who they work with, and every service provider or seller should have the right to quote their own fair price - without interference from a platform that dictates terms.

Why We Started

The Problem We Saw

Existing platforms often decide the price customers pay and dictate which service provider they are matched with. This removes choice, limits transparency, and prevents providers from offering their true value.

The Unheard Voices

Small sellers like housewives, single moms, and local artisans - people who make valuable products from home - are often left out. For them, joining large platforms is complex, expensive, and discouraging. We wanted to change that.

Our Solution

Mobitrons is built to give power back to people. Customers can choose providers and sellers they trust. Service providers set their own fair prices. Small and home-based sellers find a welcoming, simple way to reach their community.
